Simple Beam Educational Tool

(version 1.0, released Winter 2019)

by Peter Mackenzie-Helnwein
pmackenz@uw.edu

This tool allows you to interactively explore the effect of

  1. span length
  2. material choice
  3. cross section type/shape
  4. support conditions
  5. a moving concentrated force
  6. a constant distributed load

on deflection, rotation, transverse shear and the bending moment in a single span beam.
